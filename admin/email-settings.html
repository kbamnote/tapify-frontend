Setup Guide

How to configure email password

📧 Set up info@tapify.in in Hostinger:

  1. Login to Hostinger Panel → Emails → Email Accounts
  2. Click "Create Email Account"
  3. Email: info@tapify.in with strong password
  4. SSH/FTP me backend/config/email.php open करें
  5. Line: 'password' => 'YOUR_EMAIL_PASSWORD_HERE' me अपना password डालें
  6. Save → "Send Test Email" button click करें
  7. ✅ Email मिले तो सब काम कर रहा है!
