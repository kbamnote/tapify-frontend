Account Deletion

Learn how to request deletion of your Tapify account and associated data.

Users can request the deletion of their Tapify account and associated data by contacting our support team. Please follow the instructions below to initiate your request.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to:

kunalbamnote7@gmail.com

In your email, you must include the following information so we can verify your identity and process the deletion:

  • Registered Email Address: The email address associated with your Tapify account.
  • Registered Phone Number: The phone number linked to your account.

Data Deletion Process

Upon receiving your request and verifying your identity, your account and associated user data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.

What Data is Deleted:

  • Your personal profile information (name, phone number, address).
  • Your login credentials.
  • Your created digital business cards (vCards) and web stores.
  • Location logs, photos, and presentation recordings (for Tapify Sales CRM users).

What Data May Be Retained:

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Invoices and transaction history for tax and accounting purposes.
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
